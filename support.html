‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I save my calculations?
A: Calculations are automatically saved when you complete them. You'll see them appear in the "Recent Calculations" section on the home screen.
Q: How do I edit the name or add notes to a saved calculation?
A: Long-press on any saved calculation card and select "Edit Name & Notes" from the menu. You can customize the name and add personal notes.
Q: How do I delete a saved calculation?
A: Go to the "Saved Calculations" screen, long-press on the calculation you want to delete, and select "Delete" from the menu.
Q: Can I export my calculations?
A: Yes! Long-press on any calculation and choose "Export PDF" to generate a detailed report, or "Share" to send the calculation via email or messages.
Q: How do I switch between light and dark mode?
A: Go to the Settings tab and tap on "Theme" to choose between Light, Dark, or System (follows your device settings).
Q: Are the calculations accurate?
A: Our calculations use standard financial formulas and are mathematically accurate. However, they are for informational purposes only and should not replace professional financial advice.

üí° Tips for Best Results

401(k) Calculator:

  • Include employer matching in your calculations
  • Consider annual salary increases
  • Use realistic return rates (6-8% historically)

Compound Interest:

  • Regular contributions make a huge difference
  • Time is your best friend - start early
  • Consider inflation when planning

Debt Payoff:

  • List all debts with interest rates
  • Consider the avalanche method (highest interest first)
  • Factor in minimum payments for all debts

üîß Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device. Your saved calculations will not be lost.
Calculations seem incorrect
Double-check your input values. Make sure you're using the correct time periods (months vs. years) and percentage formats.
Can't see saved calculations
Saved calculations are stored locally on your device. If you've recently updated or reinstalled the app, previous calculations may not be available.
